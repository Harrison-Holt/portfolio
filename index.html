ABOUT ME

I'm Harrison Holt

a full-stack developer with a passion for building cloud-first, accessible web applications that are both user-friendly and production-ready.

I hold a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and am currently completing the UT Austin Full Stack Software Development Certificate. I’m certified in AWS (Developer & Solutions Architect) as well as digital accessibility (Trusted Tester & CPACC) — a combination that lets me build secure, scalable, and inclusive apps from end to end.

From designing AWS event-driven systems to ensuring a UI works flawlessly with a keyboard, I love turning complex problems into elegant solutions. I'm also committed to constantly improving my UI skills to make my portfolio stand out visually.

Outside of coding, I’m big into military history, and I’ll never say no to a Dr Pepper and a good book.
